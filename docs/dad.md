# Dad Persona

## Profile

- **Name:** Michael
- **Age:** 34
- **Role:** Co-parent, involved caregiver
- **Tech Comfort:** Moderate to High

## Goals

- Stay informed about baby's daily activities even when at work
- Contribute equally to tracking and caregiving duties
- Understand patterns to help with nighttime shifts
- Feel confident handling baby care independently
- Support partner by sharing the mental load

## Pain Points

- Feels out of the loop when not the primary caregiver
- Needs quick catch-up on what happened during the day
- Wants to contribute but unsure what's already been done
- Frustrated when tracking systems require duplicate entry
- Needs clear data to make decisions (Is baby due for a feed?)

## Behaviors

- Checks dashboard for daily summary
- Logs entries during evening and weekend shifts
- Appreciates data visualization and statistics
- Wants real-time sync with partner's entries
- Uses the app to prepare for solo parenting time

## Scenarios

1. **Coming home from work:** Checks app to see last feeding time and diaper change before taking over
2. **Weekend solo duty:** References typical schedule to maintain baby's routine
3. **Night shift:** Logs all activities so partner can sleep without worry and catch up in the morning
