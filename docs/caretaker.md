# Caretaker Persona

## Profile

- **Name:** Maria
- **Age:** 45
- **Role:** Professional nanny / daycare provider
- **Tech Comfort:** Low to moderate

## Goals

- Log activities accurately for parents to review
- Follow parents' preferred routines and schedules
- Communicate important observations about the baby
- Maintain professional records of care provided
- Quickly learn the app without extensive training

## Pain Points

- May care for multiple children with different tracking needs
- Not comfortable with complex technology
- Needs clear, simple interface with minimal steps
- Worried about making mistakes in the app
- Limited time for data entry while managing children

## Behaviors

- Uses app only during work hours
- Prefers large buttons and simple forms
- Needs visual confirmation that entries were saved
- May not check historical data, focused on current shift
- Appreciates preset options over free-form entry

## Scenarios

1. **Starting the day:** Checks last entries from parents to understand where to pick up
2. **During care:** Logs feedings, diapers, and naps with quick-tap interface
3. **End of shift:** Adds any notes about baby's mood or observations for parents to review
4. **Multiple children:** Switches between child profiles easily without confusion

## Access Considerations

- Limited permissions (no billing, no account settings)
- Cannot delete or modify parents' entries
- Access can be revoked when employment ends
- Activity log visible to parents for accountability
